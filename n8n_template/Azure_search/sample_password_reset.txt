Password Reset Policy - Acme Corporation

All employee accounts require a password reset every 90 days. Passwords must be at least 12 characters, include one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character.

Self-service reset:
1. Go to password.acme.local
2. Enter your Acme email address
3. Complete the MFA challenge via the Acme Authenticator app
4. Choose a new password meeting the policy above

If you are locked out and cannot access the self-service portal (e.g. MFA device lost), contact IT directly to verify identity manually. Self-service portal cannot bypass MFA for security reasons.

Locked account after 5 failed login attempts: account auto-unlocks after 30 minutes, or IT can unlock immediately upon identity verification.
